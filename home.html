Our Story

Cinemark Media Group: Where passion meets purpose.

Founded in 2023

Watsons Media Group was born from a vision shared by five innovative students at USIU-Africa. Driven by a passion for visual storytelling, these young creatives came together to forge a company that would redefine video production in Kenya and beyond.

With expertise spanning film, media, and technology, our founders built Cinemark on the principles of creativity, excellence, and commitment. Each project we take on is an opportunity to inspire, inform, and elevate.

Our Mission

To bring visions to life with engaging, high-quality content that resonates across diverse platforms. Our mission is to tell stories that not only captivate but also connect, creating lasting impacts for brands and audiences alike.

Our Vision

As a leader in media production, we aim to bridge creativity and technology, pushing boundaries to shape the future of storytelling.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Constantly pushing creative boundaries.
  • Quality: Delivering excellence in every frame.
  • Integrity: Building trust through transparent and honest relationships.
  • Collaboration: Working together for impactful results.
